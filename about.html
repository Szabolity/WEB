ABOUT

Our Story by the Sea

Aurelia was created as a quiet island hideaway where warm, genuine service meets the easy rhythm of the ocean.

Where Island Magic Feels Like Home

Tucked along the shores of Siquijor, Aurelia Haven is a family-run sanctuary designed for travelers who crave peace, sun, and a touch of seaside wonder. Every path, palm tree, and cozy corner has been thoughtfully shaped to help you unwind, reconnect, and simply breathe.

From Quiet Shoreline to Coastal Retreat

Aurelia began as a simple dream: a small slice of coastline where guests could step away from crowded cities and feel the island’s slower, softer pace.

Over the years, that dream has grown into a boutique resort filled with intimate spaces — oceanfront rooms, shaded walkways, and starlit decks — all inspired by the natural beauty of Siquijor and the warmth of Filipino hospitality.

Our team is made up of locals who know the island by heart. They’ll happily share their favorite snorkeling spots, sunset views, and stories that bring the island’s character to life.

What Matters Most to Us

Everything we do at Aurelia is grounded in the values that shaped our resort from the very beginning.

Thoughtful Hospitality

From your first inquiry to your final goodbye, we aim to anticipate what you need — and deliver it with warmth, sincerity, and care.

Quiet, Unhurried Moments

We believe rest should never feel rushed. Our spaces are designed to give you room to slow down, linger, and savor the view.

Respect for the Island

We’re committed to caring for the shoreline, waters, and community that make Aurelia possible — through mindful practices and local partnerships.

Our Mission

To create a tranquil island sanctuary where every guest feels genuinely cared for, deeply rested, and connected to the natural beauty of Siquijor. We aim to preserve the island’s heritage and environment while offering warm Filipino hospitality that leaves lasting memories.

Our Team

Aurelia is brought to life by a passionate team of locals who know the island by heart. From our front desk and kitchen staff to our housekeepers and boat guides, every member of our team works with pride, care, and genuine joy in welcoming guests.

Many of our staff have been with us for years — they are storytellers, island experts, and warm hosts who will ensure you feel at home from the moment you arrive.

More Than a Stay — It’s a Feeling

Whether you’re traveling as a couple, with friends, or with family, our goal is simple: that you leave Aurelia feeling lighter, calmer, and a little more in love with the sea.

From sunrise coffees on the deck to evening cocktails under a sky full of stars, our team is here to make every day of your stay feel easy and memorable.
