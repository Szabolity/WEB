Choose the Retreat That Feels Like You

Every room at Aurelia Haven is designed as a soft landing — crisp linens, calming coastal tones, and just the right amount of space to stretch, breathe, and watch the light drift across the water from morning to dusk.

Whether you’re here for sunrise swims, slow afternoons with a book, or barefoot evenings under the stars, there's a room that fits the pace you crave. Settle into the space that feels most like you — and let the island do the rest.
