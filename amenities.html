AMENITIES

Everything You Need to Unwind

From seaside pools to cozy lounges, every corner of Aurelia Haven is designed to help you rest, recharge, and savor your time by the sea.

Thoughtfully Curated for Slow, Beautiful Days

Whether you’re here for ocean adventures or quiet afternoons with a book, our amenities are crafted to make your stay effortless and memorable.

Seaside Infinity Pool

Take a refreshing dip with sweeping views of the ocean. Comfortable loungers, pool towels, and a nearby bar make this an easy all-day spot.

Beachfront Lounges

Settle into plush daybeds and shaded cabanas just steps from the water. Perfect for sunrise coffees and slow, golden-hour sunsets.

All-Day Dining

Enjoy fresh island flavors, comfort dishes, and crafted drinks at our on-site restaurant and bar — from breakfast through late night.

Spa & Wellness Corner

Treat yourself to massages, soothing treatments, or simple quiet time in our dedicated wellness spaces.

Island Excursions

Let our team arrange snorkeling trips, island tours, waterfalls, or snorkeling right off the shore — tailored to your pace.

Concierge Assistance

From transport to tour suggestions, our team is here to help you plan every detail of your stay with ease.

CALMING RITUALS BY THE SEA

Slow down with spa rituals inspired by the tides — warm oils, soft light, and the sound of the waves just beyond.

After a sun-drenched day, retreat to our spa corner for a restorative massage, gentle stretching, or simple stillness. It’s your moment to reset before another evening under the stars.
